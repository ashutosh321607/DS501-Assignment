Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) are one of the most world-renowned education avenues in the country. But do you know why? In this post, we will reveal the secret.Widely regarded as the most prestigious engineering institutes in the country, the popularity of these iconic institutes is not only limited to India but overseas too. These autonomous public institutions come under the purview of the Ministry of Human Resource Development and are considered as the best Institutions in the country for engineering education and research in terms of education quality and standards.
